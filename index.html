About Me

I am a data analyst and medical writer with over a year of experience in creating visualisations and conducting data analysis for medical studies. My role involves a combination of technical writing, data analysis, and client communication, allowing me to leverage my skills in Python, R, and SQL to deliver impactful results. My academic background in geochemistry and extensive experience in palaeoecological studies have provided me with a strong foundation in research and data interpretation, which I now apply in the medical field. I love learning and improving my skillset.

I hold a PhD in Evolutionary Anthropology from the Max Plank Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology, where I focused on reconstructing the past climate of western coastal Iberia using speleothems to during a time when the Neanderthals were disappearing. The aim was to better understand if climate played a crucial role in Neanderthal extinction. Under the supervision of Dr. Dirk Hoffmann and Prof. Dr. Jean-Jacques Hublin, I gained skills in geochemical processing, statistical analysis, and data visualisation, using tools such as R and Microsoft Excel. My Master’s Degree in Archaeometry from the University of Melbourne, supervised by Prof. Dr. Rainer Grün, focused on Uranium-Thorium (U-Th) dating methods.

In my current role as a data analyst and medical writer, I create visualisations using Python, R, and Excel for medical studies, write study protocols and reports, and prepare manuscripts for projects. I conduct data analysis using SQL Server and R, attend regular meetings with clients to ensure project deadlines are met and directions are agreed upon, and prepare Excel table shells and visualisations for reports and protocols. Additionally, I proofread reports prepared by non-native English speakers and perform quality assurance of results and presentations. The tools and software I frequently use include Microsoft SQL Server, Excel, Outlook, R, Python, PowerPoint, Word, and OneNote.

Since starting this role, I have obtained a Technical Writing certification and developed proficiency in Microsoft SQL Server. I have developed creative ways of presenting, discussing, and interpreting data, and enhanced my communication skills to better convey analysis to clients. I have gained a comprehensive understanding of data handling in the medical field and the bureaucratic structure of secondary medical data banks. This ongoing development has rooted me more firmly in my field.

I collaborate regularly and transparently with team members and other departments, communicating findings and data visualizations to stakeholders and clients through extensive Excel files, reports, visualizations, and PowerPoint presentations. I communicate with co-workers and clients in both English and German, further facilitating clear and effective collaboration. The company is also exploring new ways of communicating data. Looking forward, I aim to further my career in data science, work on larger projects, and continue collaborating with diverse teams. I am particularly interested in developing skills in deep learning to enhance my data analysis capabilities.
